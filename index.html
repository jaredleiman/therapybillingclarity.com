Common Questions

Frequently asked.

Is this legal advice or coding advice?

No. Therapy Billing Clarity provides general compliance information summarizing publicly available CMS, OIG, and payer guidance. It is not legal advice, not billing advice, and not a substitute for a qualified healthcare attorney or certified professional coder.

Who is this for?

Independent Licensed Clinical Social Workers (LCSWs), Licensed Professional Counselors (LPCs), and Licensed Marriage and Family Therapists (LMFTs) in private practice who bill Medicare and want a monthly briefing instead of reading CMS transmittals themselves.

How is the report delivered?

By email, on the 1st of every month. No app to install, no dashboard to log into, no software to learn.

How do I cancel?

From the Stripe customer portal link at the bottom of every monthly email. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing period.
